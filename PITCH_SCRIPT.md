# 🎤 SENTRAK — The Ultimate Pitch Script

> **Duration:** 8–10 minutes | **Team:** Flexinator (Navneeth, Sharvesh, Rahul, Uday)
> **Judges:** Startup TN + Aroopa Technologies (value business analytics, revenue, sustainability, UN goals, novelty, real numbers)
> **Scoring:** Marketing 5 | Idea 5 | Impact 5 | Implementation 2

---

## 🎬 THE PERFORMANCE BLUEPRINT

> **Tone:** Confident, conversational, slightly provocative. You are NOT begging for funding. You are offering them a seat at a table they'd regret missing. Think Shark Tank India energy — data-heavy, emotionally grounded, and dripping with conviction.

> **Rule #1:** Never read from a screen. Eye contact with every judge.
> **Rule #2:** Pause after every stat. Let it land.
> **Rule #3:** When showing the demo, hand the phone to the judge. Let THEM touch it.

>
> _(beat)_
>
> "We built SENTRAK to fix that. Permanently."

**[SLIDE 1 appears: SENTRAK logo + tagline: "The Aadhaar for Athletic Talent"]**

## ACT 2: THE PROBLEM — WITH REAL NUMBERS (90 seconds)

**[Speaker: Navneeth or Sharvesh — walks to the screen]**

> "Let me show you exactly where the system breaks."

**[SLIDE 2: "The Broken Pipeline" — 3 columns]**

> "**Problem 1: Invisible Talent.**
> Tamil Nadu alone has **12,600 village panchayats.** The state government just distributed sports kits to every single one of them under the Kalaignar Sports Kit Scheme. But here's the catch — there is **no system** to track what happens next. No data. No follow-up. No discovery. ₹718 crore in the TN sports budget, and the pipeline between 'village kid with a kit' and 'state-level athlete' is a **black hole.**"

_(pause)_

> "**Problem 2: Fake Data.**
> Right now, a coach can write anything on a clipboard. '11.2 seconds for 100 metres.' Who verified it? Nobody. We spoke to scouts in PKL and ISL — they told us point blank: **'We don't trust grassroots data. We only trust our own eyes.'** That means unless a scout physically drives to your village, you don't exist."

_(pause)_

> "**Problem 3: No Digital Identity.**
> A 15-year-old girl in Dharmapuri runs faster than 95% of her age group. She applies for the Khelo India scholarship — ₹6.28 lakh per year. But she has **zero proof.** No verified stats, no digital trail. Her application is just another piece of paper in a stack of thousands."

_(turn to judges)_

> "This isn't a tech problem. This is **India's biggest talent leak.** And it's happening right now, in every district of this state."

## ACT 3: THE SOLUTION — CRISP AND VISUAL (90 seconds)

**[Speaker: Uday or Rahul — shows the live app on a phone/projector]**

> "SENTRAK is three things:"

**[SLIDE 3: Three pillars with icons]**

> "**One — A digital Sports Passport.**
> Every athlete gets a tamper-proof profile called a **SenPass**. It contains their verified physical metrics, mental resilience scores, biometric data, and a cryptographic QR code. Think of it as an **Aadhaar card, but for athletic talent.**"
>
> _(show the SenPass card on screen — the holographic one)_

> "**Two — Offline-First Assessment Engine.**
> A coach in a zero-connectivity village opens SENTRAK on their phone. It works **completely offline.** They register athletes using voice commands — in **Tamil.** They record sprint times, agility scores, vertical jumps — all benchmarked against **SAI national standards.** When they get WiFi, everything syncs automatically."
>
> _(show the assessment flow on the demo)_

> "**Three — Witness Attestation Protocol.**
> This is our **novelty play** — the feature no competitor has. Every assessment must be co-signed by a neutral third party — a school principal, a panchayat leader — through **OTP-verified cryptographic attestation.** You cannot fake a SENTRAK score. It is physically impossible."
>
> _(show the OTP attestation modal)_

**[Hand the phone to the nearest judge]**

> "Go ahead. Try it. Tap around. It's a live PWA — works on any phone, no app store needed."
